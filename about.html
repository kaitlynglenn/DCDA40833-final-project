About Me

My name is Kaitlyn Glenn and I am a graduating senior at Texas Christian University studying Strategic Communications and Digital Culture / Data Analytics. I am from Orange County, California and have lived there my whole life.

Why I Chose this Topic

Being a Strategic Communications major, social media has become a huge topic of discussion in my classes and jobs. I am well aware of the effects of social media as I have learned not only first hand, but through academic research. I am passionate about studying more about the effects of social media on our young generation and how it shapes our culture. I have been a TikTok user for several years and have seen firsthand how it has influenced my own identity, consumer behavior, and social interactions. Through this project, I hope to explore these topics in more depth and share insights about how TikTok is shaping our digital culture.
