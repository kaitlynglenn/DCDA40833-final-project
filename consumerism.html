Section 02

Consumer Behavior

TikTok has changed the way people discover and buy products by turning entertainment into shopping opportunities. Through viral videos, influencer recommendations, and features like TikTok Shop, users are often inspired to purchase items they may not have searched for otherwise. Because the algorithm repeatedly shows products based on interests and engagement, it can shape what users want and how they make buying decisions. This section explores how TikTok influences consumer behavior and encourages impulse purchasing in digital culture.

Evidence

Survey Results

These results show that TikTok plays a strong role in shaping consumer behavior, with over half of respondents reporting that TikTok videos often influence what they want to buy and many others saying they are influenced sometimes. This suggests that product exposure on the platform is not accidental but driven by the algorithm’s ability to repeatedly show users content that matches their interests. As a result, TikTok functions not just as entertainment, but as a powerful tool that shapes purchasing decisions and encourages impulse buying in digital culture.

These results show that TikTok strongly influences product discovery across multiple categories, especially beauty products and clothing, which were the most commonly reported. This highlights how the platform’s visual and trend-driven content encourages users to explore items they may not have searched for on their own. The variety of products discovered, including books, tech gadgets, and food items, demonstrates that TikTok shapes consumer interests beyond just fashion and beauty, reinforcing its role as a powerful driver of everyday purchasing behavior.

Why It Matters

Importance

Understanding consumerism on TikTok matters because the platform is changing how people discover and purchase products in everyday life. Instead of searching for items directly, users are often influenced by trends, creators, and algorithm-recommended content that encourages impulse buying. This shift shows how entertainment and advertising are becoming closely connected online. By learning how TikTok shapes consumer behavior, users can better recognize when their purchasing decisions are being influenced and make more intentional choices about what they buy.
