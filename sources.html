Sources

This page lists the sources used throughout this project to support research on how TikTok’s algorithm shapes digital culture, identity, consumer behavior, and online communities.

Willingham, A. J., Leah Asmelash, and Scottie Andrew. “The Biggest Ways TikTok Has Changed American Culture.” CNN, 2 Apr. 2023, https://www.cnn.com/2023/04/02/us/tiktok-american-culture-effects-cec/index.html

Maheshwari, Sapna, et al. “Love, Hate or Fear It: TikTok Has Changed America.” The New York Times, 18 Apr. 2024, https://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2024/04/18/business/media/tiktok-ban-american-culture.html

Economist Impact. “Reshaping E-Commerce Strategy.” Economist Impact, https://impact.economist.com/trade-geopolitics/reshaping-ecommerce-strategy

Rosenblatt, Kalhan. “Women Who Spend Time on TikTok Feel Less Satisfied With Their Bodies, Study Suggests.” NBC News, 7 Aug. 2024, https://www.nbcnews.com/health/womens-health/study-women-tiktok-body-image-rcna165267

Zaveri, Jui. “TikTok and the Death of the Attention Span.” The Oxford Blue, 23 May 2023, https://theoxfordblue.co.uk/tiktok-and-the-death-of-the-attention-span/

Suriyapong, Apichai T. “The Aesthetic and Narrative Strategies of Subcultures on TikTok: Analyzing the Presentation of Niche Identities.” Studies in Social Science & Humanities, vol. 3, no. 9, 2024, pp. 42–47, https://www.paradigmpress.org/SSSH/article/view/1331

Wang, Xin, and Qian Shang. “How Do Social and Parasocial Relationships on TikTok Impact the Well-Being of University Students? The Roles of Algorithm Awareness and Compulsive Use.” Acta Psychologica, vol. 248, 2024, article 104369, https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38936231/

Biswas, Ahana, Alireza Javadian Sabet, and Yu-Ru Lin. “Toxic Politics and TikTok Engagement in the 2024 U.S. Election.” Harvard Kennedy School (HKS) Misinformation Review, 20 Aug. 2025, https://misinforeview.hks.harvard.edu/article/toxic-politics-and-tiktok-engagement-in-the-2024-u-s-election/

Sheikh, Mahnoor. “How the TikTok Algorithm Works in 2026.” Sprout Social, 6 Feb. 2026, https://sproutsocial.com/insights/tiktok-algorithm/

Burgess, Matt. “All the Ways TikTok Tracks You and How to Stop It.” WIRED, 23 Oct. 2021, https://www.wired.com/story/tiktok-data-privacy/
