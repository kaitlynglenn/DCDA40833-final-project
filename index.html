Final Project

Shaped by TikTok

TikTok is more than an entertainment platform. It shapes identity, influences consumer behavior, affects political awareness, builds communities, and encourages parasocial relationships between users and creators. This project explores how one platform shapes modern digital culture.

Research Focus

Research Question

How does TikTok’s algorithm shape cultural trends, personal identity, and everyday decision-making in digital culture?

Hypothesis

Because TikTok’s algorithm prioritizes personalized and engaging content, it strongly influences what users watch, believe, and buy, shaping modern cultural trends, identity formation, and everyday decision-making in digital spaces.

Overview

About This Project

Purpose

This project examines TikTok as a cultural influence rather than just a social media platform. Each section explores a different way the app affects everyday decisions, identity, and social interaction.

What This Site Explores

The following pages investigate how TikTok shapes beauty standards, consumer trends, political understanding, niche communities, and relationships between creators and audiences.

Approaching the project

Methodologies

This project uses a mix of qualitative and quantitative research methods, including: insights from academic studies, analysis of TikTok trends, case studies of viral content, and a user survey to understand how TikTok shapes culture.

To create my website, I used AI to help guide me on the layout and colorings of certain sections to fit the aesthetic of the project. It was a helpful tool to get a general idea of how I wanted the website to look, but I made sure to customize the design and content to fit my vision for the project.

Why It Matters

Why study TikTok?

Because TikTok’s algorithm curates personalized content streams, it influences not only what users watch but how they think, interact, and participate in culture. Understanding TikTok helps explain how digital platforms shape modern social behavior.
