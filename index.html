Shaped by TikTok

This website presents how TikTok’s algorithm shapes cultural trends, personal identity, and everyday decision-making in digital spaces. It also explores how features like the For You Page and TikTok Shop influence what users watch, believe, and buy, helping audiences better understand the platform’s role in modern digital culture.

About This Project

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to help viewers better understand how TikTok’s algorithm influences what people watch, believe, and buy in their everyday lives.

What You'll Learn

The goal is to increase awareness of how digital platforms shape identity and culture, and to encourage more thoughtful and informed social media use.

Research Question and Hypothesis

Research Question

How does TikTok’s algorithm shape cultural trends, personal identity, and everyday decision-making in digital culture?

Hypothesis

Because TikTok’s algorithm prioritizes personalized and engaging content, it strongly influences what users watch, believe, and buy, shaping modern cultural trends, identity formation, and everyday decision-making in digital spaces.
